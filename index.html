Benefits

Yoga can have many physical and mental health benefits, including: Flexibility: Yoga poses improve flexibility and range of motion. Strength: Yoga can help build muscle strength and tone. Balance: Yoga can help improve balance and stability. Posture: Yoga can improve posture and awareness of how you sit and stand. Stress management: Yoga can help reduce stress and anxiety. Mental clarity: Yoga can help improve concentration and focus. Sleep: Yoga can help improve sleep quality. Heart health: Yoga can help lower blood pressure and heart rate. Weight management: Yoga can help with weight management by improving metabolism and eating habits. Immune system: Yoga can help boost immunity. Pain management: Yoga can help relieve chronic pain conditions like arthritis, headaches, and lower back pain.

Traditional yoga

Traditional yoga practice takes a holistic, 360-degree approach to health and wellness, focusing on physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual aspects. It emphasizes the union of mind, body, and soul, creating harmony among all three.

Hatha yoga

Hatha yoga is a school of yoga that focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breath control (pranayama) to balance the body, mind, and spirit, and is often considered an umbrella term for many common yoga styles practiced in the West. It involves slow, deliberate movements, holding poses, and mindfulness, aiming to improve flexibility, strength, balance, and relaxation.
